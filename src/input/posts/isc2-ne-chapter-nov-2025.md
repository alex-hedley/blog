<!-- # ISC2 North East England Chapter November 2025 Meetup -->

Come and join our in person event on 11 November at the Newcastle Building Society office at Cobalt Business park.
The ISC North East Chapter welcomes all ISC2 members, anyone working in cyber / infosec and students. Come join us for an evening to network and the usual: talk, talk, pizza, talk format.

The theme for the evening: Challenging Times in Cyber Security.

Category: Science & Tech, Science
